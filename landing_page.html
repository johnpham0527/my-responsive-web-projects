Plan Your Visit

Browse

Hunters Point Community Library will have over 50,000 new books, periodicals, and other print materials. Several thousand items are available in Chinese, Spanish, Russian, Japanese, and other languages.

Read

Enjoy Hunters Point Community Library's dedicated reading spaces for adults, teens, and children's areas.

Learn

Hunters Point Community Library has a ground floor meeting room that can seat up to 140 people. The space will host dozens of educational and cultural programs a month for all ages, including those from Queens Public Library's first-ever environmental education center.

Stroll

Inside the library, warm bamboo creates an inviting space and natural light enters through the large glass cut-outs in the sides of the building, offering spectacular views of the East River, the United Nations, and the east side of Manhattan on one side and the growing community of Long Island City on the Other. View the Percent for Art Installation by artist Julianne Swartz.
